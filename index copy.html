    What qualities and skills are essential for a successful cadet officer?

    1. What are the key responsibilities of a cadet officer?

  • Leadership Development: Cadet officers are often tasked with leading and mentoring junior cadets, helping to develop their skills and instilling discipline.
  • Training and Drills: Participating in and sometimes leading training exercises, drills, and simulations to prepare for real-world scenarios.
  • Physical Fitness: Maintaining a high level of physical fitness and participating in regular physical training sessions.

They also deal with intense training scenarios, such as exposure to chemical agents and the pressure of performing under scrutiny.
